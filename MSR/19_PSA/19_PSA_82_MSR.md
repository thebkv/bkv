# Psalm 82 — Authority Judged from Within
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Psalm-Specific)

Psalm 82 is not about mythology, angels, or rival deities.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this psalm reveals how **delegated authority collapses when it forgets its source and purpose**.  
The judgment described is not external punishment, but **structural exposure**: authority failing to perform its assigned function.

The psalm operates entirely at the level of **governing responsibility**.

---

## Core MSR Principle for Psalm 82

> **Authority exists to uphold justice; when it does not, it forfeits legitimacy.**

---

## Psalm 82 — MSR Summary (Non-Editorial)

- Authority is **real**, but **delegated**
- Authority is **conditional**, not absolute
- Authority is judged by **function**, not status
- When authority fails, **Source reclaims governance**
