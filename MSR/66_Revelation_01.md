- **Jesus Christ** → **Anointed / Unified Consciousness**
- **Angel** → **Messenger Faculty (Symbolic Transmission)**
- **John** → **Spiritual Awareness (Receptive Perception)**

- **Asia** → **Realm of Lived Experience**
- **Blood** → **Life-force / Self-giving Presence**
- **Clouds** → **Veiled Perception**
- **Alpha & Omega** → **Source and Fulfillment**
