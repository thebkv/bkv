# Genesis 05 — Continuity of Identity Across Generations
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Genesis 05)

Genesis 05 is not primarily about biological descent or lifespan curiosity.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter functions as a **continuity register**:
it records how *identity*, *likeness*, and *authority* are transmitted, degraded, or preserved across successive internal states.

The repetition is intentional.
The structure teaches **what persists**, **what changes**, and **what inevitably ends**.

---

## Core MSR Principle for Genesis 05

> **What is generated carries the likeness of its generator, and what is not re-aligned eventually concludes.**

This chapter establishes:
- inheritance of likeness
- mortality as a system condition
- one preserved exception (Enoch)
