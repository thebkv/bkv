# Genesis 47 — Provision Without Freedom  
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Framing for Genesis 47

Genesis 47 is not primarily about famine logistics or ancient economics.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter examines what happens when **survival is secured without corresponding freedom**.  
It shows how provision, when centralized, can quietly convert relief into dependency.

This chapter completes a sequence:
- **Genesis 45–46**: Reconciliation and relocation
- **Genesis 47**: Stabilization under external authority
- **Genesis 48–49**: Reassertion of inner inheritance and identity

Genesis 47 is the **cost ledger** of rescue.

---

## Core MSR Principle for Genesis 47

> **Provision that bypasses maturity consolidates power elsewhere.**
