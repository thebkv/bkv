# Genesis 09 — Authority Re-Granted and Life Bounded
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Orientation (Genesis 09)

Genesis 09 does **not** reset creation.  
It **re-authorizes life after collapse**, but with **explicit constraints**.

Under MSR, this chapter establishes:
- renewed authority
- bounded dominion
- stabilized accountability
- remembrance mechanisms that prevent future total collapse

This is **post-judgment governance**, not innocence restored.

---

## Core MSR Principle for Genesis 09

> **Authority without boundaries leads to collapse; authority with bounds sustains life.**
