# Genesis 23 — Possession Through Covenant Purchase
## MSR Architectural Reading (Narrative + Inner)

---

## MSR Preface (Genesis 23)

Genesis 23 is the chapter where **promise becomes lawful possession** without conquest.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, the chapter functions as an inner pattern:
- grief is honored (not denied),
- the future is secured (not seized),
- and inheritance enters the world through **covenant and payment**, not force.

Everything is interpreted by what it *does* in the sequence.
