# Genesis 15 — Promise Anchored Beyond Performance
## MSR Architectural Reading

## MSR Preface (Genesis 15)

Genesis 15 does not establish obligation.
It establishes **assurance**.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter answers a single question:

> How is continuity secured when visible fulfillment is absent?

The answer is not effort, conquest, or moral perfection —
but **alignment secured by Source initiative**.

This chapter introduces:
- Promise without immediate evidence
- Trust without performance
- Covenant without negotiation
