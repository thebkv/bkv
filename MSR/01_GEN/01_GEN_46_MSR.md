# Genesis 46 — Descent Without Loss of Identity
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Frame for Genesis 46

Genesis 46 does **not** introduce a new covenantal action.  
It governs **transition**.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter stabilizes a critical movement:
the descent of the promised line into Egypt **without forfeiting identity, promise, or authority**.

The danger is not relocation.
The danger is **absorption**.

This chapter exists to prevent that.

---

## Core MSR Principle (Genesis 46)

> **Movement into constraining environments must be sanctioned, named, and bounded by Source.**

Without this step, Genesis would fracture.
