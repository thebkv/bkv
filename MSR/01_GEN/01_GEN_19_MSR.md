# Genesis 19 — Judgment as Exposure, Escape as Alignment
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Genesis 19)

Genesis 19 is not primarily about:
- sexual ethics
- urban corruption as a sociological category
- divine anger as emotional reaction

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter functions as:

> **The exposure and collapse of a misaligned collective state once separation from Source is complete.**

Judgment here is not arbitrary punishment.  
It is **what happens when a system can no longer sustain coherence**.

Rescue is not favoritism.  
It is **alignment responding to warning**.

---

## Core MSR Principle (Genesis 19)

> **When a system rejects mediation, exposure becomes inevitable.  
> Escape requires decisive separation without negotiation.**
