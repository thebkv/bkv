## MSR Preface (Genesis 04–Specific)

The Genesis 04 narrative introduces historicaal genealogy.  
Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter reveals what happens **after order is established** when **orientation diverges**.

The text traces:
- two modes of offering (orientation toward Source vs. orientation toward output),
- the rise of comparison,
- the birth of resentment,
- and the first internal fracture that results when correction is refused.

Everything is interpreted **only by the role it performs in the sequence**.
