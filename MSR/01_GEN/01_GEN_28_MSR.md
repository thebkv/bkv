# Genesis 28 — Alignment Re-established Through Encounter
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Genesis 28)

Genesis 28 does **not** describe a random mystical dream or a sudden religious experience.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter records the **re-establishment of alignment** after displacement.  
It shows how **Presence asserts continuity** when identity is unstable, unprotected, and in transition.

The chapter’s role is to demonstrate that:
- Alignment does not depend on location
- Authority can be conferred before maturity
- Presence precedes understanding

Nothing here requires:
- dream mysticism
- angel hierarchies
- speculative cosmology

Every element is read **only by what it stabilizes in the sequence**.

## Core MSR Principle (Genesis 28)

> **Alignment is initiated by Presence, not by preparedness.**
