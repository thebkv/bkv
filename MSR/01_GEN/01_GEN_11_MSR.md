## MSR Preface (Genesis 11–Specific)

Genesis 11 is not primarily about language diversity, geography, or ancient construction.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, Genesis 11 reveals what happens when **unified human capacity** attempts to establish **self-originating authority** apart from Source.

The confusion of language is not punishment.
It is **containment**.

The scattering is not rejection.
It is **preservation**.

---

## Core MSR Principle for Genesis 11

> **Unified power without alignment collapses into fragmentation.**
