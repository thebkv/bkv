## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Genesis 45)

Genesis 45 is not about emotional closure or family reconciliation alone.  
Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter functions as the **moment of revealed identity** after long-tested concealment.

The chapter answers a critical question in the Joseph sequence:

> What happens when authority, wisdom, and provision are fully formed — and the past finally confronts the present?

Genesis 45 shows that **true authority reveals itself without accusation** and **restores without erasing responsibility**.

---

## Core MSR Principle for Genesis 45

> **Revelation of identity precedes restoration of relationship.**

Healing cannot occur while truth remains hidden — but truth revealed without maturity would destroy rather than heal.
