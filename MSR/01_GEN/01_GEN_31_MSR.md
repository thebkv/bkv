# Genesis 31 — Separation with Accountability
## MSR Architectural Reading (Two-Column Format)

## MSR Preface (Genesis 31)

Genesis 31 resolves a **necessary separation** that has been forming silently.
Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter governs **exit without collapse**:
how aligned identity disengages from an exploiting system **without theft, revenge, or erasure**.

This is not family drama.
It is the **protocol for lawful departure**.

---

## Core MSR Principle (Genesis 31)

> **Alignment must separate from exploitation without becoming exploitative.**

Separation succeeds only when:
- truth is spoken,
- boundaries are named,
- and authority is formally transferred.

## MSR Summary (Genesis 31)

Genesis 31 teaches that:

- Exploitation must be **named**, not endured.
- Separation must be **lawful**, not reactive.
- Alignment exits **clean**, leaving judgment to Source.
- Boundaries prevent future harm.

This is how identity leaves a system **without becoming it**.
