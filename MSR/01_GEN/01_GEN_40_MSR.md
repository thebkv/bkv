# Genesis 40 — Discernment Retained in Confinement  
## MSR Architectural Reading (Two-Column)

---

## MSR Preface (Genesis 40)

Genesis 40 does not advance the outer plot of ascent.  
It **tests whether inner discernment remains intact when elevation is delayed**.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter examines:
- whether interpretation still functions under restriction
- whether truth is spoken without leverage
- whether alignment depends on outcome

This is **discernment under confinement**, not promotion.

---

## Core MSR Principle for Genesis 40

> **True discernment does not require freedom, reward, or visibility to operate.**
