## MSR Preface (Genesis 29)

Genesis 29 examines how **desire**, **labor**, and **misrecognition** interact before stable authority emerges.  
Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter is not about romance or trickery alone, but about how **right desire** can be delayed, redirected, and disciplined until it produces enduring structure rather than impulse.

The chapter functions as a **formation-through-delay** sequence.
