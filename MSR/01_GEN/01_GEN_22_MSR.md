## MSR Preface (Genesis 22)

Genesis 22 is not a lesson in cruelty, blind obedience, or arbitrary demand.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter reveals the **final testing of trust after promise fulfillment has begun**.  
It examines whether alignment with **Source** is conditional upon outcomes—or whether trust persists **even when meaning is temporarily withdrawn**.

This chapter does **not** teach:
- child sacrifice
- moral paradox as virtue
- suspension of conscience

It reveals:
- trust tested beyond comprehension
- obedience without explanation
- provision revealed *only after release*

Every element is interpreted **only by the role it performs in the sequence**.
