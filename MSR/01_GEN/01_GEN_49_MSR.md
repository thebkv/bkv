## MSR Preface (Genesis 49)

Genesis 49 is not fortune-telling, astrology, or tribal myth.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, Genesis 49 functions as a **terminal alignment speech**:
a final evaluation of how distinct inner faculties have actually *operated* over time.

Jacob does not assign futures.
He **names consequences**.

Each “blessing” reveals:
- how a faculty handled authority,
- whether it stabilized order,
- and what role it can carry going forward.

This chapter explains why leadership, stability, and inheritance are distributed **unevenly**.

---

## Core MSR Principle for Genesis 49

> **Authority is allocated according to demonstrated function, not birthright.**
