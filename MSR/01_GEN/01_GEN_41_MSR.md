# Genesis 41 — Discernment Elevated, Authority Delegated
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Genesis 41)

Genesis 41 is not primarily about dreams, famine, or political rise.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, Genesis 41 functions as the **transition point where tested discernment is formally entrusted with governing authority**.

What has been:
- preserved through restraint (Gen 39),
- refined through injustice (Gen 40),

is now **recognized, elevated, and delegated** for the sake of collective survival.

The chapter establishes a core MSR rule:

> **Interpretive clarity without self-interest is the prerequisite for legitimate authority.**

---

## Core MSR Principle for Genesis 41

> **Authority is delegated only when discernment proves faithful to Source rather than advantage.**

Dreams here are not mystical spectacles.
They are **compressed signals requiring aligned interpretation**.

## MSR Summary (Genesis 41)

Genesis 41 establishes that:

- Discernment matures in obscurity
- Authority recognizes clarity only when systems fail
- True interpretation does not self-claim
- Authority is delegated, not seized
- Governance exists to preserve life, not elevate ego

**Minimum Sufficient Role Statement:**

> Genesis 41 reveals how discernment, once proven faithful to Source and restraint, is entrusted with authority for the preservation of many.
