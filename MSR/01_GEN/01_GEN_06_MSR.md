# Genesis 06 — Corruption, Grief, and the Preservation of Alignment
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Frame for Genesis 06

Genesis 06 describes **systemic corruption of human consciousness** and the necessity of **preserving an aligned pattern** so ordered life can continue.

This chapter is not about:
- supernatural hybrids
- divine rage
- annihilation for its own sake

Under MSR, Genesis 06 reveals:
- how **misaligned authority spreads**
- how **discernment collapses**
- why **reset becomes necessary**
- and how **one aligned pattern** preserves continuity
