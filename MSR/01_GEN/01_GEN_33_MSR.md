## MSR Preface (Genesis 33)

Genesis 33 resolves the internal tension built across Genesis 27–32.  
Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter functions as **restored relationship without loss of identity**.

No dominance is asserted.  
No guilt is leveraged.  
No roles are erased.

Reconciliation here is **functional alignment**, not emotional theater.
