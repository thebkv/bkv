# Genesis 03 — The Emergence of Fracture and the Birth of Moral Consciousness
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Genesis 03)

The Genesis 03 narrative reading describes the invention of evil, and the fall of a perfect world into chaos.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, Genesis 03 reveals the moment **undifferentiated obedience gives way to self-referential evaluation**.  
The chapter documents how **moral consciousness emerges**, how **authority is displaced**, and how **fracture becomes experiential**.

Nothing here requires:
- a literal talking animal
- inherited guilt theory
- mythological inflation

Everything is read **only by the role it performs in the sequence**.

---

## Core MSR Principle for Genesis 03

> **Fracture begins when evaluation replaces trust.**

The issue is not knowledge itself, but **knowledge seized apart from alignment**.
