# Matthew 03 — Preparation Before Authority Appears
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Matthew-Specific)

The **conventional reading** of Matthew 03 understands this chapter as a call to  
religious ritual, moral reform, and public spectacle.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, Matthew 03 functions more precisely as the
**necessary preparation phase before divine authority can be revealed and recognized**.

This chapter establishes:
- alignment before appearance  
- orientation before empowerment  
- humility before authority  

Nothing here is performative.
Nothing here creates authority.

Everything prepares the ground so authority can *stand*.

## MSR Integrity Statement (Matthew 03)

Matthew 03 teaches:

- Authority is preceded by preparation.
- Alignment comes before empowerment.
- External order must yield to internal truth.
- Authority submits before it leads.
- Recognition follows obedience.

Nothing more is required for the chapter to function.
Nothing less explains why authority is accepted when it appears.
