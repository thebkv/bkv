# Matthew 01 — Authority Introduced Through Lineage
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Matthew-Specific)

**Conventional Reading:**  
Matthew 01 is traditionally read as a genealogy establishing Jesus’ legal descent from Abraham and David, followed by the account of a miraculous birth validating messianic identity.

**MSR Reading:**  
Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, Matthew 01 functions as an **authority map**, not a family record.

This chapter explains **how legitimate authority enters history without bypassing history**.

It shows:
- how authority is transmitted through real structures  
- how alignment can persist through failure  
- why divine authority does not arrive detached from consequence  

The genealogy is not ornamental.  
Every name appears because it **carries or breaks authority in a specific way**.

Nothing here is symbolic excess.  
Nothing is accidental.

---

## Core MSR Principle for Matthew 01

> **Authority must be lawful, traceable, and tested before it can be exercised.**

Matthew does not begin with miracles.  
He begins with **structure**.

## MSR Integrity Statement (Matthew 01)

Matthew 01 teaches:

- Authority must enter through structure.
- Promise does not bypass failure.
- Collapse does not cancel legitimacy.
- Restoration is quieter than domination.
- Divine authority honors law before exercising power.

Nothing more is required for the chapter to function.  
Nothing less explains why Matthew begins here.
