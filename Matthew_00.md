# Matthew 00


### **Cast of Matthew 1–2 (Non-Genealogy)**
| Person / Group | Conventional Meaning | BKV Meaning (Inner Faculty / Function) |
|----------------|----------------------|----------------------------------------|
| **Mary** | Beloved; sea; wished-for child | **Holy Receptivity** — the faculty that conceives the Word when the heart yields. |
| **Joseph** | “He will add”; Yahweh increases | **Structural Faithfulness** — obedient alignment that protects what God plants. |
| **Jesus** | “Yahweh saves” | **Revealed Deliverance** — the awakened Divine Identity at the center of the soul. |
| **Immanuel** | God with us | **Felt Presence** — awareness of union; God perceived within. |
| **Angel of the Lord** | Messenger | **Breakthrough Intuition** — revelation entering the soul with direction. |
| **Herod** | (Possibly) hero-like | **Egoic Self-Preservation** — the false king resisting the rise of Christ-identity. |
| **Magi** | Wise men; interpreters of signs | **Seeking Intellect** — highest reason following the inner star toward truth. |
| **Chief Priests & Scribes** | Religious authorities | **Inert Knowledge** — truth known but not acted on; doctrine without movement. |
| **Rachel** | Ewe; mother figure | **Grieving Tenderness** — sorrow over lost potentials during transition. |
| **Archelaus** | Ruler of the people | **Inherited Ego Pattern** — the echo of the old self still trying to govern. |
| **Children of Bethlehem** | Infants | **Innocent Potentials** — emerging spiritual possibilities threatened by the ego. |
