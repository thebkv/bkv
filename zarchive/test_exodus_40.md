# Exodus 40: The Presence
## Alignment Completed & Presence Installed

---

## The Spine: The Shift of Authority
- **Completion confirms obedience.** - Presence arrives without human invitation once the structure is **aligned**.
- The role of the disciple shifts from **building** to **following**.

---

## VBV — Exodus 40:34-38
| KJV | BKV MSR |
| :--- | :--- |
| Then a cloud covered the tent... | Presence enters without invitation once alignment is complete. |
| The glory of the LORD filled the tabernacle. | Indwelling confirms operational readiness. |

---

## Symbolics: Functional Realities
- **Cloud:** Manifest Presence providing guidance and restraint.
- **Glory:** Full activation of divine indwelling.
- **Journeying:** Movement directed by Presence rather than impulse.

---

## SDR: When the Work Stops
> "The cloud does not ask permission. It fills the structure because everything is ready. This is the moment when effort ceases and guidance begins."
