This folder tracks recurring **biblical symbols** as structural elements within the Bible Key framework.

These symbols appear throughout scripture and often represent **states, forces, environments, or functions within the inner life of a disciple**.

By observing how these symbols behave across many passages, patterns emerge that help reveal the deeper architecture of the biblical narrative.
