# Matthew 26 — DOSSIER

## Spine Summary
Matthew 26 governs the **transition from instruction to surrender**, where aligned awareness consents to loss of control while every supporting structure fractures. Authority is not taken by force here, but preserved through voluntary yielding.

---

## NR — Narrative Reading (Name-Meaning Oriented)

Jesus (Aligned Sonship) announces that the appointed passage is at hand. The inner teacher does not resist what is coming, but names it clearly.

The religious system, centered in Jerusalem (Seat of Peace turned administrative), plots preservation through removal. Power seeks to stabilize itself by eliminating the living signal.

A woman approaches with costly ointment. She recognizes value before understanding outcome. Devotion is poured out without calculation. Practical minds object, but aligned awareness affirms the act. Some things are discerned only by love, not efficiency.

Judas (Self-Interest) detaches. The inner fracture accepts a price. Betrayal is not dramatic. It is transactional.

At the shared table, the aligned life reframes memory itself. Bread becomes participation. The cup becomes consent. Covenant is internalized, not enforced.

In Gethsemane (Oil Press), pressure reveals orientation. Natural desire recoils, yet deeper alignment yields. The will bends without breaking.

Disciples scatter. Structures fail. Witnesses distort truth. Authority is mocked, struck, and restrained.

Peter (Faith-in-Action) denies alignment under threat. The rooster sounds. Awareness remembers itself through sorrow.

The chapter ends suspended. No resolution yet. Only consent remains intact.

---

## MSR — Minimum Sufficient Role
This chapter establishes that **alignment is preserved through voluntary surrender, not resistance**, and that every external support will fail before the inner orientation is confirmed.

Nothing here is taken.
