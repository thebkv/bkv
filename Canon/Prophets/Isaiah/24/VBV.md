# Isaiah 24 — VBV

## Summary

Isaiah 24 describes the collapse of a corrupted world-system and the exposure of the underlying violation that caused it. Human structures—social rank, economy, celebration, and governance—lose their stability because the foundational order of life has been broken. The chapter shows that the disruption affects every level of society equally. As the system unravels, joy disappears, cities fail, and the environment itself becomes unstable. Yet within the collapse a remnant emerges that recognizes the majesty of the Lord. The chapter concludes with the removal of false authorities and the re-establishment of the Lord’s rule from Zion, restoring rightful governing order.
