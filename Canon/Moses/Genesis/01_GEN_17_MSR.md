# Genesis 17 — Identity Marked, Promise Stabilized
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Genesis 17)

Genesis 17 does not introduce a *new* promise.
It **formalizes** and **stabilizes** an already-given promise by marking identity.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter establishes:

- the **transition from promise to identity**
- the difference between **belief** (Genesis 15) and **embodied alignment**
- the role of **signs** as boundary-markers, not causes

The covenant here is not transactional.
It is **identity-defining**.
