## MSR Preface (Genesis 37)

Genesis 37 introduces **calling before character**, **vision before maturity**, and **identity revealed before integration**.  
Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter does not explain jealousy, family psychology, or ancient customs.  
It establishes what happens when **revealed identity outruns internal coherence**.

> **Core MSR Principle:**  
> Revelation without integration provokes resistance—both internally and externally.
