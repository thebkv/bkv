## MSR Preface (Genesis 07)

Genesis 07 does not describe destruction for its own sake.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter records the **transition from discernment to execution** — the moment when ordered consciousness **enters preservation** because unrestrained patterns can no longer be stabilized in place.

Judgment here is **functional**, not emotional.  
The flood is **reset pressure**, not punishment.
