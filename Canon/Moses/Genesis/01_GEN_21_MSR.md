# Genesis 21 — Fulfillment Without Forgetting the Cost
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Local to Genesis 21)

Genesis 21 completes a long-delayed promise while **refusing to collapse fulfillment into sentimentality**.

Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter shows that:
- Fulfillment does not erase prior conflict
- Promise does not cancel consequence
- Laughter and separation can coexist without contradiction

The chapter holds **three parallel movements**:
1. **Promise fulfilled**
2. **Illegitimate mixture resolved**
3. **Authority clarified with external powers**

Each section stabilizes a different layer of identity.

---

## Core MSR Principle for Genesis 21

> **True fulfillment completes a promise without undoing the disciplines that made it possible.**
