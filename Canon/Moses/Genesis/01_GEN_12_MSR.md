## MSR Preface (Genesis 12)

Genesis 12 marks a **structural pivot** in Scripture.

Up to this point, the narrative has shown:
- ordered creation (Genesis 1–2)
- fractured alignment (Genesis 3–11)
- repeated failure of collective authority when unified power is not aligned with Source

Genesis 12 introduces a **new stabilizing strategy**:
> Authority is no longer rebuilt through collective systems, but through **a single aligned trust-response**.

This chapter does **not** describe ethnicity, nationalism, or biological privilege.  
Under MSR, it reveals how **right orientation to Source restores forward movement** after systemic failure.
