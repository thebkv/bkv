# Genesis 34 — Violation, Vengeance, and Misaligned Zeal
## MSR Architectural Reading

---

## MSR Preface (Genesis-Specific)

Genesis 34 is not a moral endorsement of violence, nor a tribal defense narrative.  
Under **Minimum Sufficient Role (MSR)**, this chapter exposes what happens when **injury is real**, **judgment is bypassed**, and **zeal acts without alignment**.

The chapter functions as a **warning case**:  
> *Right concern pursued through wrong authority fractures the household and destabilizes inheritance.*

---

## Core MSR Principle for Genesis 34

> **Justice pursued without alignment becomes corruption, even when the grievance is legitimate.**

## MSR Summary (Minimal and Complete)

Genesis 34 reveals that:

- Injury does not authorize unchecked retaliation  
- Sacred language can be corrupted by zeal  
- Justice requires **alignment + authority**, not outrage alone  
- Misaligned zeal fractures the household more than the original offense  

> **The chapter exists to warn future authority-holders what not to do.**
