# Biblical Character Database — Abraham
v 3/9/2026

## Core Meaning
- Father of a multitude
- Foundational faith
- Departure from old identity into covenant identity

## Name Meaning
- Abram: exalted father
- Abraham: father of a multitude

## First Functional Role
- The man called out from old ground into promise
- The beginning of covenantal reorientation

## Character Pattern
- Called
- Trusts
- Falters
- Learns
- Receives promise
- Becomes father-pattern

## Positive Symbolism
- Faith
- Obedience
- Departure from the familiar
- Covenant alignment
- Willingness to move before full visibility

## Negative or Shadow Risk
- Fear-based compromise
- Attempting to fulfill promise through fleshly means
- Partial trust

## Key Scenes
- Call out of Ur
- Egypt episode
- Lot separation
- Covenant promise
- Hagar and Ishmael
- Name change
- Offering of Isaac

## Bible Key Reading
- Abraham represents the state where a person hears a higher call and leaves the old internal country.
- He is the beginning of inner reordering by trust.
- His failures do not cancel the call, but reveal the places where trust is still incomplete.

## Notes
- Abraham is not merely “a man of faith” in a generic sense.
- He is the architectural beginning of ordered promise-consciousness.
