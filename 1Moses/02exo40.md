# Dossier: Exodus 40
**Subject: Alignment Completed and Presence Installed**

---

## BKV Spine

### Spine Summary

Exodus 40 concludes the construction sequence by moving from preparation to indwelling. Every component has already been formed, positioned, and tested. This chapter does not introduce new instruction; it confirms obedience. When alignment is complete, Presence fills the structure without human initiation. Authority does not enter gradually or partially. It arrives fully once conditions are met. The tabernacle becomes operative not because it is impressive, but because it is aligned.

### Disciple Orientation

- Completion matters as much as obedience.
- Presence fills what is fully aligned, not what is partially assembled.
- The work stops when Presence arrives.
- Guidance continues from within once alignment is established.

## SDR / RFM — When the Work Stops

For the disciple, Exodus 40 explains what happens after obedience is complete.

Nothing new is said. Nothing new is built. Moses simply finishes. And then Presence arrives.

The cloud does not ask permission. It does not wait for ceremony. It fills the structure because everything is ready. This is the moment when effort ceases and guidance begins.

From this point forward, movement is no longer driven by anxiety or planning. The people move when Presence moves. They stop when Presence remains.

Exodus 40 teaches that alignment is not an endless process. There is a moment when the work is done and the Presence takes over. When that happens, the role of the disciple shifts from building to following.
