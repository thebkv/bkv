# Dossier: Genesis 34
**Subject: Zeal Without Alignment**

## BKV Spine

### Spine Summary
Genesis 34 exposes the danger of zeal detached from alignment. Moral outrage without discernment fractures what promise was meant to preserve. Justice pursued without authorization produces collateral damage.

### Disciple Orientation
- Outrage is not authority.
- Action without alignment multiplies harm.
- Protection of identity requires restraint.
