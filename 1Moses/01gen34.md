# Dossier: Genesis 34
**Subject: Misaligned Zeal and Its Cost**

## BKV Spine
Genesis 34 exposes the danger of violent correction when alignment is replaced by outrage. Zeal without discernment fractures the system it claims to defend.

## VBV — Minimum Sufficient Role
Violation occurs. Response escalates beyond justice into destruction. Authority is compromised through uncontrolled retaliation.

## Symbolics
- **Dinah** — vulnerable exposure
- **Shechem** — coercive assimilation
- **Sons of Jacob** — unchecked reactive force

## SDR / RFM — When Anger Leads
For the disciple, Genesis 34 warns that defending identity through rage corrupts the very promise it seeks to protect.
