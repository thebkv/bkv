# Dossier: Genesis 38
**Subject: Preservation Through Irregular Means**

## BKV Spine
Genesis 38 interrupts the Joseph narrative to show that preservation continues even through moral and structural irregularity.

## VBV — Minimum Sufficient Role
Judah fails in stewardship. Tamar secures continuity through discernment and courage.

## Symbolics
- **Judah** — leadership under testing
- **Tamar** — rightful preservation
- **Signet and staff** — authority exposed

## SDR / RFM — Preservation Finds a Way
For the disciple, Genesis 38 reveals that Source integrity is not halted by human failure.
