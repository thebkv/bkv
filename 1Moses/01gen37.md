# Dossier: Genesis 37
**Subject: The Descent That Preserves the Line**

## BKV Spine
Genesis 37 begins the Joseph cycle, showing how favor provokes rejection and descent becomes the means of preservation.

## VBV — Minimum Sufficient Role
Dreams reveal future authority. Envy distorts perception. Joseph is removed from the family system to preserve it.

## Symbolics
- **Dreams** — revealed trajectory
- **Pit** — descent without death
- **Coat** — visible favor
- **Sale** — misjudged loss

## SDR / RFM — The Descent Is Not the End
For the disciple, Genesis 37 shows that being cast down can be the beginning of preservation.
