# Dossier: Genesis 07  
**Subject: Entry into Containment and Release of Pressure**

---

## BKV Spine

### Spine Summary

Genesis 7 records the moment when preservation moves from preparation to activation. What was instructed in Genesis 6 is now enacted without delay or deviation. The aligned steward enters the refuge, boundaries are sealed, and corrective pressure is released. This chapter establishes that containment is not symbolic or partial. Once the system crosses the threshold of instability, separation is enforced. Preservation does not argue with collapse. It proceeds.

### Disciple Orientation

• Alignment must act before pressure becomes visible.  
• Preservation requires separation, not negotiation.  
• Containment is initiated by obedience, not fear.  
• Once sealed, the system must run its full course.

## SDR / RFM — Genesis 07  
**When the Door Closes**

For the disciple, Genesis 7 is about timing and trust.

Noah enters the ark before the rain begins. Nothing has changed in the environment yet. The sky still looks the same. The ground still holds. But alignment does not wait for confirmation. It moves when instruction is given.

Then the pressure comes. Not gradually. Not selectively. The deep breaks open. The heavens release. Everything outside containment is overtaken. The system is not repaired. It is overwhelmed.

The most important line in the chapter is simple. Source shuts the door. Preservation is no longer exposed to external conditions. Noah does not control this moment. He does not reopen it. Containment belongs to Source alone.

Genesis 7 teaches the disciple that there are moments when alignment must already be inside the refuge. Once pressure is released, there is no partial entry. Separation has occurred.

If you feel enclosed tonight, remember this. Being sealed is not punishment. It is protection while the old structure dissolves.

Containment holds.
