# Dossier: Genesis 40
**Subject: Interpretation Before Elevation**

---

## BKV Spine

### Spine Summary

Genesis 40 establishes that authority is exercised in obscurity before it is recognized in public. Alignment expresses itself through faithful service and accurate interpretation while outcomes remain delayed. The chapter shows that gift precedes position, and discernment operates even when circumstances appear stalled.

### Disciple Orientation

- Gift operates before promotion appears.
- Faithfulness is proven without visibility.
- Interpretation serves others before it serves the self.
- Delay does not indicate abandonment.

## SDR / RFM — Faithful in the Waiting

For the disciple, Genesis 40 teaches patience under correctness.

Joseph is accurate. He serves well. His discernment is confirmed. None of this changes his situation immediately. Alignment does not demand instant results.

Interpretation is given to serve others, not to escape circumstance. When remembrance fails, faithfulness continues.

Genesis 40 shows that delay is not denial. It is staging. Authority is being prepared quietly through accuracy, restraint, and service, even when outcomes do not move.
