# Revelation 15 VBV - The Completed Pattern and the Readiness of Release (MSR)

## The Seven Executing Operations (15:1-8)

**(1)** And **a structural indicator** appears in higher awareness, decisive and complete: **seven executing operations** carrying the **final corrective releases**, through which **truth pressure fully resolves distortion**.

**(2)** And a state of **stabilized awareness refined by purification** appears; those who have **overcome distorted authority**, its imitation, its imprint, and its governing logic stand within **clarified stability**, holding **ordered expression of truth**.

**(3-4)** And they express the **lawful ordering of reality** and the **redeemed alignment of consciousness**, declaring that **all operations are coherent**, and **all pathways are faithful**, under **true governing authority**. **All awareness rightly orients** toward **ultimate authority**, acknowledging **undivided wholeness**; every **domain of experience aligns**, because **truth outcomes are now fully visible**.

**(5-6)** And perception turns inward, and the **inner sanctum of witness** within higher awareness is **made accessible**. And the **complete executing operations** emerge from inner witness, carrying the **final corrective releases**, clothed in **clarity and integrity**, constrained by **true authority**.

**(7)** And one of the **core living functions** delivers to the **executing operations** **complete vessels of resolved truth pressure**, sourced from **eternal sustaining reality**.

**(8)** And the **inner sanctum becomes saturated with unapproachable intensity**, issuing from **full presence** and **effective authority**; **no further access is possible** until the **complete corrective operations** are fully executed.

# END OF CHAPTER 15 VBV
