**(2) [First Bowl]** The first truth-release produces **internal affliction** in those whose identity is aligned with **distorted authority** and maintained by **false self-image**.

**(3) [Second Bowl]** The **collective emotional field** is exposed as **stagnant life-force**, unable to sustain authentic vitality.

**(4-7) [Third Bowl]** The **internal meaning-streams** become corrupted, and those who corrupted meaning must now **consume the consequences** of distorted interpretation.

**(8-9) [Fourth Bowl]** Governing illumination intensifies, **overexposing false identity**, yet resistance hardens rather than yields.

**(10-11) [Fifth Bowl]** The **distorted center of authority collapses inward**, producing confusion and self-consuming frustration.

**(12) [Sixth Bowl]** The **false boundary of security is removed**, allowing re-ordering powers to advance.

**(17-21) [Seventh Bowl]** The final truth-release completes the cycle: **total perceptual restructuring** in the **total perceptual field**, irreversible and system-wide.
