BACKGROUND — GALATIANS

AUTHOR  
The letter was written by Paul the apostle. He writes personally and forcefully, grounding his authority not in institutional appointment but in direct calling and revelation.

DATE  
Galatians was likely written around AD 48–55, during Paul’s early missionary period, shortly after the establishment of the Galatian churches and before later, more systematic letters such as Romans.
